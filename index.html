How This Is Meant To Be Used

A simple operating rhythm that works for both service calls and project-based jobs.

1. Intake every opportunity
Even small service calls belong in the system so follow-ups and collection risk stay visible.
2. Keep stage and next action current
Those two fields power the action center, daily board, and cash forecast.
3. Track estimated and actuals together
That closes the loop between selling, delivering, and protecting margin.
4. Record paid amounts the same day
Your collected cash and A/R become accurate enough to actually manage from.
Built for network field technicians, low-voltage crews, electricians, HVAC, plumbing, remodeling, landscaping, cleaning, handyman work, and other contractor workflows that mix quoting, scheduling, dispatch, and field execution.
